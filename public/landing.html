What Drova does
01
Live GPS tracking link
The moment a driver starts a run, your customer gets a link showing exactly where the truck is on a live map — including a continuously updated ETA. No app, no login — just a link.
02
Automatic SMS alerts
Customers get a text when the truck departs and again when it's close to arrival. You choose how many minutes notice they get.
03
Dedicated driver app
Drivers use a purpose-built app on their own phone — reliable background GPS that keeps running even with the screen locked on long hauls.
04
Full job history
Every run is logged — distance travelled, delivery time, customer details. Access it from your dispatcher dashboard anytime.
05
Automatic invoice generation
Set a rate per kilometre or per hour on each job. The moment a delivery is marked complete, Drova generates an invoice automatically — ready to send or push directly to Xero.
